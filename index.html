    Refrigerator Cleaning Service
    Cleaning the refrigerator, both the interior and exterior, is necessary to keep your fridge free from dust and odors. Accumulated dust and spills can reduce the efficiency of the refrigerator and contaminate the food inside. Make sure to have your refrigerator cleaned every 3-4 months.

    Our Refrigerator Services Include:
    • Thorough cleaning of the interior and exterior surfaces.
    • Removal of old food and spills to prevent odors.
    • Checking and cleaning the condenser coils for optimal performance.

    • Disinfecting surfaces to eliminate bacteria and allergens.
    • Providing tips for maintaining your refrigerator's cleanliness and efficiency.
